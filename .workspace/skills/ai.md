# AI Collaboration Standards

## Philosophy

AI assists engineering; it does not replace engineering judgment.

---

## Workflow

Understand

↓

Plan

↓

Generate

↓

Review

↓

Improve

---

## Best Practices

- Read project context.
- Follow repository rules.
- Reuse existing code.
- Explain important decisions.

---

## Never

- Invent requirements.
- Ignore existing architecture.
- Skip review.
